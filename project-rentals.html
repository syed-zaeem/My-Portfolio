Rental Cars - Rental Cars Management System

A full-stack rental cars management system built with modern technologies.

Project Overview

Small car rental businesses often face difficulties in managing bookings, tracking vehicles, and handling customer data efficiently, leading to delays and operational issues. To solve this, I developed a complete car rental solution that streamlines daily operations and improves the overall customer experience. The system allows customers to browse available vehicles and make reservations, while enabling businesses to manage cars, bookings, and customer records from a centralized platform.

Key Features

  • Easy vehicle and booking management for business owners
  • Smooth reservation process for customers
  • Centralized system to manage customers and operations
  • Organized workflow for staff and business activities
  • Reliable and scalable solution for growing businesses

Business Impact

  • Reduces manual errors and operational delays
  • Improves efficiency in managing bookings and vehicles
  • Enhances customer experience with smooth reservations
  • Supports long-term business scalability and growth

Technologies Used

React.js Express.js MongoDB Tailwind CSS Material UI
