Shop - Grocery Management System

A full-stack grocery management system built with modern technologies.

Project Overview

Small grocery businesses often face challenges in managing inventory, handling orders, and serving customers efficiently, while customers prefer the convenience of online shopping. To address this, I developed a complete online grocery management system that simplifies daily operations and provides a smooth ordering experience. The platform allows customers to browse products and place orders, while enabling business owners to manage inventory, track orders, and handle customer interactions from a single system.

Key Features

  • Simple and efficient product and inventory management
  • Easy order placement with a smooth user experience
  • Centralized system to manage customers and orders
  • Responsive design for use on mobile and desktop
  • Reliable platform for day-to-day business operations

Business Impact

  • Reduces manual effort in managing daily operations
  • Improves customer convenience with online ordering
  • Helps businesses operate more efficiently
  • Supports business growth with a scalable system

Technologies Used

React.js Express.js MongoDB Tailwind CSS Material UI
